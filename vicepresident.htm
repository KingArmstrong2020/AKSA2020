About Armstrong:



My name is Steven Armstrong. I grew up in Texas. I bounced around in foster care, ending up with a mormon family, a protestant family, and then catholic family. I am deeply religious, and love christianity.

I attended Brigham Young University and received a double degree in religious studies and law.

I fought in Iraq and later was elected Senator of Texas in 2004, and was reelected in 2010. I retired in 2016 to be community leader and a family man.

Steven Armstrong lived in Austin as well as Texas.
