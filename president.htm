Annie King

Architects are generally paid well. The average salary of an American architect is $76,000 a year. The lowest yearly wage for an architect is around $46,000, and the highest is around $125,000. The median hourly wage is $36.59, starting from $22.15 to $60.34. The highest paying places to work as an architect are New York and California.
